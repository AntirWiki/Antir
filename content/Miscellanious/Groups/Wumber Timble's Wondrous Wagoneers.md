![[WumberPoster.png|right|300]]Wumber Timble's Wondrous Wagoneers are a group of circus performers that travel across [[Antir]]. Their show, which travels by wagon as the name suggests, includes numerous attractions; strange beasts and spirits, performers of all sorts, games and prizes, and more. 

Their leader, **Wumber Timble**, is a gnome with a natural panache and an eye for talent. He loves his circus, but isn't afraid to cut some corners or make questionable deals to keep it afloat.
