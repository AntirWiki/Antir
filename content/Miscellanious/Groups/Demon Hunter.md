The organized [[Spirits#Demons|demon]] hunters of [[Antir]] are led by [[Vada]], whose primary mission is to hunt down the demonically-possessed [[Defied]].

# Wulvs
During the age of [[New Fontia]], the corrupted priests of the [[Font|Fontian]] discovered [[Werewolf|Werewolves]] living within the walls that fled with the original refugees. The demon leader persecuted these werewolves, and eventually pushed them outside of the city. [[Athanasius]] claimed the werewolves could not defy, and thereby living proof of blasphemy. The 15 werewolves were ejected from the community and forced to live out their remaining days in the mountains. As a final act of care, The Font did something completely uncharacteristic. She expended the last of her energy to grant the werewolves what they believed she had the power to grant them. The power of defiance. In doing so, she expended herself, and was fully corrupted by demon influence. But, with that energy, the werewolves became Wulvs – as named by Victoire. The werewolves’ spirits became empowered by the last of The Font’s energy, and became untethered to their original bodies. Now, when a willing vessel chooses, the spirit of a Wulv can affix themselves to the person and grant them the ability to defy. When they defy, the spirit of the original Wulv is reborn, and granted complete control over the body. Victoire would like me to note that each Wulv has a preferred weapon hand crafted by the lady, which she bestows upon them upon their rebirth. (I believe this is to help her denote who is who). During the age of New Fontia, the Wulvs prowled the city's outskirts, protecting it from outsiders and those from Antir who would threaten to kill them. Many held out hope that even the people who condemned them could be saved by The Font’s light. Now, some Wulvs continue to protect the ruins of New Fontia, while others join Vada in the hunt for demons.

## Bestial Oath
An oath a demon hunter can make to their leader Vada. An oathed one promises to relinquish their body after death, and is used in the ritual of defiance to resurrect a Wulv.
