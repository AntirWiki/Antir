A demon forged from the fear of broken trust and shattered alliances. Summoned by a member of The Church of [[Lumina|Lucent]] during the [[Three Kings War]]. The summoner is unknown. Athanasius took control of The Fontian by replacing their head mother priestess, and is responsible for the shackling of [[Font|The Font]].
