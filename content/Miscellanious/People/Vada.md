Current demon hunter and former defiance priest of [[Font|The Font]]. Victoire travels under the name Vada, and trains wards to hunt the [[Defied]] who have spread throughout Antir in the modern age. It is speculated that Victoire holds the final positive connection to The Font, and feeds off spiritual energy to sustain a life well beyond her years. Trains both hunters and those who have taken the [[Demon Hunter#Bestial Oath|Bestial Oath]] to serve as arms after their death and defiance.
