A current demon hunter and former defiance priest of [[Font|The Font]]. Victoire travels under the name Vada, and trains wards to hunt the [[Defied]] who have spread throughout Antir in the modern age. It is speculated that Victoire holds the final positive connection to The Font, and feeds off spiritual energy to sustain a life well beyond her years. Trains both hunters and those who have taken the [[Demon Hunter#Bestial Oath|Bestial Oath]] to serve as arms after their death and defiance.
%%
Demon hunter, queller, hand of balance, jailor of demons, and scourge of pandemonium. Vada spends her time both in Antir proper when she has a willing host as a chess player of the demon incursion, and in the demon spirit realm as an agent when she's a corporeal spirit. I think she absorbs or becomes the embodiment of the spirit of the Font, and when she died as a human she transcended to spirit/demon status. On Antir, she pilots a vast organization of demon hunters with the mission of protecting people from highly violent, demon controlled Defied, and allowing demonic spirits and Defied who maintain slices of their humanity to exist within Antir monitored by her team so they don't do any harm. She is feared by the church, by the Defied as a police force of sorts (i.e. fuck around and kill people, and Vada will send you back to the demon sea), by demon spirits in the spirit sea and islands (I think she fucked with them and torments them to be better, and or shape them a bit to her will. I think she believes if she can make demons fear her, she can alter their personalities in slight ways to make them less true evil). I also thinks she just causes problems in the spirit world when she's not physically manifested in Antir. Her organization would then search for a person willing to serve as a vessel for resurrection, but she can also show up as a regular apparition since there is a lot of fear around her.

I also think she forces her wards to be afraid of her as well, so if there isn't anyone else to be afraid, they will be innately and she'll stick around. A light traumatizing of fear, like they're all afraid of what she's capable of, which serves as a safety net for her existence.
%%
