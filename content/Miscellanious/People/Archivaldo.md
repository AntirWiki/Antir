Once a mortal who stood up to [[Athanasius]], now a demon spirit born from the fears of other [[Spirits#Demons|Demons]] and [[Font|Fontian]] alike. Archivaldo brought ruin to [[New Fontia]] hidden away in the mountains, and caused their reintegration with society. His spirit is demon in nature now, and has the ability to be reborn whenever a Fontian dies. Although, he likes to stay low–unless Athanasius has been reborn as well.
