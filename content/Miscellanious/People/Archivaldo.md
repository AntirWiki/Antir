Once a mortal who stood up to [[Athanasius]], now a [[Spirits#Demons|Demon]] spirit born from the fears of other [[Spirits#Demons|Demons]] and [[Font|Fontian]] alike. Archivaldo brought ruin to [[New Fontia]] hidden away in the mountains, and caused their reintegration with society. His spirit is demonic in nature now, and has the ability to be reborn whenever a Fontian dies. Although, he likes to stay low–unless Athanasius has been reborn as well.

# History
Once a citizen of New Fontia, and close to Vada before they both turned spirit and demon, Archiavolo was a boy who Defied with an intensely powerful demon, and completely eradicated the demons identity instead of fusing with it. A demon so afraid of what Archi wanted to do that it decided to peace off existence because of the world Archi would inevitably create. Archi obtains the magic of the demon spirit and retains his entire identity. Archi is a plague to New Fontia and leads a revolution against its ruler (Athan). Archi's goal is to destroy the church of Antir for what the curse of Defiance they inadvertently put on his people, and then to cull the Fontian bloodline from Antir to prevent demons from ever defying again.
