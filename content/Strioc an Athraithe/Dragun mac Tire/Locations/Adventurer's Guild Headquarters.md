![[GuildHQ.jpg|right|500]] The headquarters of the [[Adventurer#The Adventurer's Guild|Antiran Adventurer's Guild]] is located in [[Dragun Mac Tire]]. It is a grand building, with many towers and crenellations and enough twisting corridors to become lost in.

It houses several key parts of the guild. Records of all registered adventurers, past and present, are found alongside the many tomes of the guild library. The building also houses the many relics that have been donated to or acquired by the guild, including the **Keystone Ledger** - the great message board that links the Grand Ledgers of guildhalls across Antir.

A dormitory within the building is available to all guild members, frequently used by those resting between quests or training at one of Dragun mac Tire's many educational institutions.

The building is the center of guild leadership, and [[Adventurer#Guildhearts|Guildhearts]] frequently meet here to consult with each other and make decisions for the Guild as a whole. It also serves as a sort of city hall for Dragun mac Tire, since the guild is the only governing body in the town.

Not least, it serves as a hub for quests across [[Strioc an Athraithe]], which is one of the most active regions for adventurers.

# People
**[[Rosaline and Kastenia Valentine]]** - Founders of the guild. The sorcerous twins still lead the organization, but Kastenia has not made a public appearance in many years. 

**Kurogh** - A guild official who helps manage newer members. A large orc man who wears fine outfits and reading glasses.
