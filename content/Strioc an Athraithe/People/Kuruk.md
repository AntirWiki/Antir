Kuruk is the [[Spirits#The Fae|Fae]] guardian of the the [[Ravenwood]], the forest bordering the [[Silent Woods]].

Around 1350 BR, he had a relationship with local Wisdom [[Yarrow Nightingale]], and fathered [[Thistle Nightingale]] - though Yarrow died in childbirth.

# Description
Incredibly tall, with a pale elf-like face and dark wings that form a cloak around them when not in use. They do not speak, instead projecting emotions and ideas into the minds of those nearby that they wish to communicate with.
