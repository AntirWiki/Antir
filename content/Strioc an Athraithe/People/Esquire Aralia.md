Esquire Aralia of the Grasping Vine is a fae spirit living in [[Strioc an Athraithe]]. They are a low-ranking member of the Spring Court, holding domain over vines that trip people.

# Description
The Esquire appears as a two-foot tall ball of green vines, with a face and limbs also made of vines. They typically appear with their subjects, a number of other vine spirits of similar appearance.

# History
Aralia met the [[Elmhollow Party]] shortly after the group left [[Elmhollow]], mistaking them for allies of [[Juniper Beaumont]]. After clearing up the confusion, they gifted the party a Cold Iron sword and their blessing.
