The Silent Woods is a forest located to the north of [[Alturon]], along the border between the [[Lucentia]] and the [[Strioc an Athraithe]]. All life that enters the woods is eventually ended, an effect that increases in potency the further one ventures in. Larger beings such as humans can survive for a few days under this influence, while lesser creatures such as small animals have been known to spontaneously dismember soon after entering. 

Trees and other plants within the forest appear completely dead, yet somehow continue to grow as if they still lived. New trees can even grow from seeds, though they never gain a single leaf. Despite the lack of any leaf-bearing trees, there is still a light covering of dead leaves across much of the forest floor.

There have been sightings of strange necrotic creatures living amongst the dead trunks, and legend says that some witches who followed the Autumn King - twisted by their master, and now known as [[Witch|Hags]] - still live within the forest's borders.

The trees around the Silent Wood, while not dead like those within it, are still withered and gnarled. Locals call this area the [[Ravenwood]].

# Residents
Scoth the Child-Flenser, [[Hag]].

# History
The woods were created in 1077 BR when [[Autumn King]] was defeated and the [[Lich]] [[Kyrillos]] was separated from him. Legend says that the lich's ruined soul is housed in the great oak tree that stands in the center of the forest.
