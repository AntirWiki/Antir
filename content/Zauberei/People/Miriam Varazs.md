Miriam Varazs is the headmaster of [[Foxfire Academy]] and Vox Arcana of [[Zauberei]]. 

She is originally from [[Blackwing]], though her family originates among the [[Skell]] of the southern mountains. She moved to Zauberei with her younger sister [[Irina Varazs]] when Irina was admitted to Foxfire. The two remained close, and Miriam was the natural choice for Headmaster when her sister became Archmage.

# Description
She is a Half-[[Orc]] woman, with a medium build and dark hair in a bob cut.
