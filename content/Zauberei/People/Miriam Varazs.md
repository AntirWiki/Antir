Miriam Varazs is the headmaster of [[Foxfire Academy]] and Vox Arcana of [[Zauberei]]. 

She is originally from [[Blackwing]], though her family originates among the [[Skell]] of the southern mountains. She moved to Zauberei with her sister [[Amalia Varazs]] when Amalia was admitted to Foxfire.

# Description
She is a Half-[[Orc]] woman, with a medium build and long dark hair.
