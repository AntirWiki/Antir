General Lie Konketsu is the commander of the Zauberan military and Vox Aegis on the city-state's ruling council. He is half [[Moondancer]] and half human. He originally joined the Zauberan army to fight against the [[Owl Nation]], seeking retribution for their mistreatment of those with mixed ancestry, though he has since lost much of that fervor.

# History
His mother was a lower-class [[Moondancer]] and his father was an [[Adventurer]]. His mother was exiled after his birth, for which he holds a deep grudge against the [[Owl Nation]].

He never knew his father, but holds little resentment for that - though it has given him a somewhat low opinion of adventurers. "I know adventurers are allergic to responsibility, and he probably didn't even know it was possible for me to be born."
