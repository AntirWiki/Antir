The Keyshadows are a mysterious group of assassins that operate in the [[Holy Kingdom]]. They are servants of [[Rhylea]], goddess of secrets. Their daggers bear upturned key symbols, representing that each death is a locked secret never to be revealed.

# History
In 1020 BR the Keyshadows saved the life of Queen [[Aisling of Antir]] when she was attacked in [[Alturon]] by followers of the newly born [[Verita]].
