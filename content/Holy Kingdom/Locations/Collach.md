Collach is a small town south of [[Alturon]], sitting on the crossroads of the main roads east to the [[Strioc an Athraithe]] and south to [[Blackwing]]. It is primarily a hunting town, but also deals with some commerce from the roads.

The town is named after its guardian, a great nature spirit that takes the form of a boar.

# History
[[Tooth and Claw]] visited Collach during their investigation of [[General Tanner]]. There they met [[Wisdom Sawyer]] and learned that the town's guardian spirit had been corrupted. 

After traveling through the final battlefield of the [[Three Kings War]], they quelled the beast and cured it of the demonic corruption Tanner inflicted upon it.
