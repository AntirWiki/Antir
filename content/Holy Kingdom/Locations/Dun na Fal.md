Dun na Fal is a fortress within the [[Holy Kingdom]], located just west of [[Alturon]]. There is also a town outside the fort, referred to by the same name.

# History
Fort Dun na Fal was the site of a battle during the Three Kings War between [[Lasairian of Antir|Lasairian's]] forces and those of the northern alliance. During this battle, Drake general [[Darios O'Mael]]'s bonded dragon Velns was slain, causing the general to enter an indiscriminate rampage. The aftermath of this ultimately led to his surrender and capture by northern forces.

Following the Three Kings War, former southern leader [[General Tanner]] infiltrated the castle with Siv, a young gnoll cleric of Tival and former healer for Faolan of Antir. After learning the truth about [[Cianan of Antir]]'s murder of Faolan, the fort nearly entered rebellion against the crown until [[Tooth and Claw]] persuaded them to halt their rebellion and killed Tanner.
