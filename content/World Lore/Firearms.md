Firearms, also known as guns, are a new technology to Antir, arising in [[Blackwing]] less than a century ago. Though that nation's agents work tirelessly to prevent their spread beyond its borders, the weapons have still begun trickling into other parts of the continent.

The technology is still in its infancy, and the vast majority of guns can only fire a single shot before requiring a lengthy reload. Still, Blackwing has incorporated them heavily into the [[Blackwing Legion|Legion]], and their battlefield presence has helped the country retain their famed martial superiority.

Guns and the powder that fuels them can be bought in Blackwing with relative ease, but obtaining either - especially in large quantities - is almost impossible anywhere else. According to hushed rumors, Blackwing agents seek out and assassinate any person trying to distribute firearms outside of their home country.

# Gunpowder
Brightpowder?
Shockpowder?
Blastpowder?
Gunpowder is the substance that fuels firearms in Antir. It has the unique ability to absorb energy of almost any type, slowly changing color as it does so - from a deep black to a bright white.

When a pile of gunpowder is touched by silver - a natural disruptor of magic - it releases all its stored energy in a single burst of light, force, and heat. Firearms aim to control this property, packing charged gunpowder into the barrel and unleashing its energy on command with silver firing pins. The resulting explosion propels the bullet at high speeds, and the remaining gunpowder - now black and un-charged - is usually collected to be reused.

Legion quartermasters typically charge gunpowder by leaving it in the sun for a set amount of time. This method is slow, but reliable in a nation with minimal clouds and even less rain. While it can be charged in other ways - even thrown in a fire, in an emergency - these are less consistent and harder to perform at large scale. The powder is graded based on hours spent in the sun, on a scale that typically ranges from one to ten - after ten hours, even the purest gunpowder stops absorbing energy.

Spent gunpowder can usually be traded for charged powder with a small fee. Many ammunition vendors will also offer a discount if the buyer returns their previously spent powder.

The only known source of gunpowder is a mine in the [[Great Gates]] region of the Blackwing desert.

%%
Unknown to most, gunpowder is actually the ground-up form of a substance known as Godsbone. Many believe the material to be the bones of a long dead spirit - perhaps even a god - that was once sacred to the people of [[Taluvhas]]. Countless of these bones lie under the Great Gates, though it is difficult to tell if they are from many creatures or a single impossibly large one.
