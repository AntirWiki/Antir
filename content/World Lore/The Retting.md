_**SOMEWHERE. SOMETIME. NIGHT? DAY? SUMMER? WINTER? SPRING? FRACTALS OF A MESSAGE. THE MOMENT OF YOUR DEEPEST VULNERABILITY.**_ 

_~Flashes of images. Skeletons of leaves. Plants reduced to vasculature upon jagged spikes of wood and flakes of ash. Unsettling pieces of words spoken by something not quite human and stitched together with little regard to sound or structure. A message rickety and wrought with reckless abandon. What does it mean? Is it truth? Is it fallacy? A devil’s temptation… a gods answered prayer? Are you special, or just a victim of circumstance? Is there meaning? Is it a dream? An acute episode of psychosis? An overactive imagination…? It’s hard to tell.~

**_How does one define_ “True Madness” _…?_*** 

Is it a disease? A rotting fungus wyrming its way into the mind until spores sprout and the roots are too deep to pull? A slow and burning thing. Bubbling up beneath the surface. But that is something to be treated with herbs and a watchful eye. 

It is not _“True”_. 

Is it a curse? The spit of a god in the eye of one so tethered to the earth? Forever given visions of ambition too grand to ever reach from the confines of a soul chained to Alturon’s soil. But that is something to be prayed about, grounded in earthly work, and drowned in holy water. 

It is not _“True”_. 

Alturon has had its fair share of “Mad Gods”. 
The poor and anguished Hunt. 
The Schism of the Lucent. 
The Banishment of the Black Autumn. 
Each one with a devout camp on either side eager and ready to argue whether one was right or wrong in the end. All with stories and clerics and sparks of humanity. 

They were not _“Truly” Mad_.

True Madness….is that beyond herb and faith healer. That not of alchemical or acolyte. The kind where the mind is fractaled beyond thought and the body rendered nothing but a living puppet…. The kind beyond rational discussion or cult following…. 

And it is the result of a choice. 
A _known_ decision. 
The idea of seeing the downward spiral in front of you as it appears over and over again, and always sprinting forward with no hesitations until there is nothing left of yourself, and those you drag with you, to turn back and choose a different path. 
The cycle of self-destruction generating a consciousnessless, endlessly seeking energy. A force outside of the confines of mortal and gods that knows only hunger. A Thing slithering through the Weave of not the divine, natural, or arcane. A sycophant made of chaos and entombed in aging oak. An immortal manifesto with no following. No human hand to pull the idea into something conceptual. Desperate to know itself. To pour into something else that can give it any sort of shape. Can direct the gnawing need for power. Only vague remnants of memories and brief sight through multiple eyes to understand its own purpose. 

**THAT** is true Madness.

_**That is the Plight of The Retting.**_ 

Scholars have said somewhere in some dusty book in some dusty library that evil yearns to be known. Is this evil? Is it the birth of something new from the root of unconscious evil? Or is it something else entirely… 

Regardless, it can be assuredly said that Alturon has grown too comfortable with the simplicity of avoiding a blackened tree in the woods. Slowly, but surely, something will tip the balance and the answers to what such a purely distilled force will wreak upon being given back access to sentience….Prince? or Parasite… All will be revealed in time. 

_Fey & Magus. 
A shattered past. 
Distilled and Dormant. 
A shadow was cast._ 

_Magic & Madness. 
Retting and Rot. 
A tree lies silent. 
With all that’s forgot._
