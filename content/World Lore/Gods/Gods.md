Gods in this world are less clear-cut than one may think. The difference between "Powerful faery", "Local guardian spirit", and "god" is mostly dependent on context and the way these beings interact with the world. The beings referred to officially as "Gods" are those spirits that embody more abstract concepts like law, or theft, or war. More naturally-oriented powers are often called spirits or fae.

The spirits revered as Gods typically reside in [[Tir na Nog#Elysium|Elysium]], alongside their servants the [[Tir na Nog#Celestials|Celestials]]. However, many great spirits from other planes are revered as Gods as well.
