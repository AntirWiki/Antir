Anairi is the secret god of soldiers in [[Lucentia]]. They represent martyrs and those who do thankless work.

Though [[Ceartas]] is the official patron of the Lucentian military, many soldiers do not relate to the idealized soldier - a righteous defender of good - that he represents. They instead turn to Anairi, who is more relatable to soldiers who have seen the harsh realities of war, and seems to understand the ugly things often required of a soldier.

According to some myths, Anairi is coupled - or even married - to [[Rhylea]], goddess of secrets. The pair represent the “necessary evils” of secrets and violence, a direct contrast to [[Lumina]]’s philosophy of truth and mercy. Followers of Anairi often give small tribute to Rhylea, since the soldier’s god is typically honored in secret.
