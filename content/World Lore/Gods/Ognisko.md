Ognisko is a goblin god of tinkerers and risk-takers. 

The firemaster is said to bless some followers with a very brief prescience - just far enough to know if the action they're about to take will cause disaster. This boon is traditionally used by alchemists and bombsmiths, allowing them to try risky ideas that others wouldn't dream of attempting.
