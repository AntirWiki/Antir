Lamaera is the god(dess) of love, passion, and obsession in [[Lucentia]]. They represent not only lust and romance, but passion in all its forms - be it for a person, a craft, a cause, or anything else.

Fluid in gender, she is said to appear as whoever you most desire - which can be a lover, but could also be a proud mentor, a treasured child, or anyone else the viewer would want to see.

Due to the passion commonly held by artists and artisans, his church is the largest patron of the arts in Lucentia.

Usually depicted as a beautiful or handsome dwarf
