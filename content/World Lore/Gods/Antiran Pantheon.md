#lore
 
Religion is highly valued in the society of the [[Holy Kingdom]], and they worship several official gods (and a number of unofficial ones as well). Some of their number are listed below. These are just some of the gods worshipped in the Holy Kingdom, and many parts of Antir recognize different pantheons altogether

## **[[Lumina]]**
Goddess of light and mercy 
- Leader of the pantheon and patron of the royal family
- Formerly known as Lucent before the schism between Lumina and [[Verita]]

## **The Grey Lady**
goddess of thieves, urchins, and the downtrodden 

## **Raeldan**
god of history and discovery (twin and rival to Rhylea) 

## **[[Rhylea]]**
goddess of secrets and mystery (twin and rival to Raeldan) 

## **Soln**
The Silent Hunter, god of the woods and the hunt 

## **Tival**
god of sacrifice 

## **Lamaera**
goddess of love, passion, and obsession
