The labyrinth lord is the god of the [[Minotaur]] in northern Antir.

# Stonesmiths
A Minotaur Undermaze is led by a Stonesmith and their priests. The Stonesmiths are the only minotaurs that can fully see in the dark, can communicate with the other Minotaur stonesmiths, and can communicate directly with the Lord of Labyrinths. Stonesmiths are marked at birth for their station, with bright glowing yellow eyes. They are the only magic users among the minotaurs. The priests are more mundane.
