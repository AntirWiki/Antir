Frostpetal is a flower common in [[Antir]] that blooms in snowy weather. Its leaves, when smoked or brewed, have a calming effect.

It is not native to Antir, but was introduced when the Antirans migrated from the [[Valley of Cold Sun]]. It is said that it was used to counteract the maddening effect of that region's sunlight.
