Defiance: A ritual conducted at the death of a [[Font|Fontian]], orchestrated by the corrupt Font and the demon [[Athanasius]]. When a Fontian approaches death, their skin explodes into a gruesome flower of flesh and gore. A [[Spirits#Demons|demonic]] spirit is pulled from the abyss by the corrupted Font, who acts as a conduit and pathway between the abyss and mortal world. The spirit of the demon fuses with the spirit of the human, and the remaining amalgamation is known as a Defied. Small features are known to change during Defiance. For starters, new skin is grown around the muscle as the previous flesh is discarded. This skin takes on a new color, usually jet black, pale white, purple, or a shade of red. Rarely does the skin look natural.

They do not suffer from aging or mortal needs like food or rest. The spirit takes control of the body, while an imprint of the former human remains. The human resides in the mind as a conscious copilot watching the actions of the demon. This transformation allows a demon to ascend its base needs and move on to something greater. Impulses are controlled by the human’s mind, and demons can form bases of operation, religions and cults, as well as gain political influence.
