#creature
Dragons are massive reptilian creatures native to the continent of [[Antir]]. They are varied in form, but most are able to fly in some way and all possess a breath weapon, the exact nature of which varies greatly.

They are immortal, steadily growing as they age. Young dragons are about a dozen feet in length, and elder dragons have been recorded at over 30 feet long.

Dragons are obsessive creatures, known for hoarding - often specific types of material objects or wealth, but sometimes more ephemeral concepts such as power, influence, or magic.

# History
The first dragons were demigods created through the sanctification of mortal Drakoi during the age of the [[Drakos Empire]]. They served the Drakos gods and their followers until some Drakos began worshipping the dragons themselves. This caused the demigods to become more powerful, and led to the lust for power seen in many dragons to this day. When the empire collapsed, the Dragons were all that remained.

Centuries passed, until the Antirans migrated from the north. The dragons would only accept them as servants, so the Dragon Wars began. Ultimately, through mortal innovation and teamwork (and the dragons' refusal to cooperate with their fellows), the dragons were driven into [[Taluvhas]] and the mountainous border of the two continents.

After their interactions with mortals as allies of [[Lasairian of Antir]]'s army during the [[Three Kings War]], dragons began spreading north once more. These dragons - many of whom were not alive for the era of the Dragon Wars - were able to tolerate the presence of nearby mortals, and live mostly in solitude all across the continent. Rumor states that some have even secretly infiltrated mortal societies by donning magical disguises.

# Dragon-Seers
Dragon-seers are mysterious spellcasters from the south of Antir, rumored to have similar bonds to dragons as the leadership of Lasairian's army during the Three Kings War.

# Notable Dragons
Ozymandias, black dragon, companion to Lasairian of Antir during the Three Kings War until the death of both during the final battle in 952 BR.

[[Dilitrius]], green dragon, master of [[General Tanner]] during and after the Three Kings War and mastermind of the 953 BR siege of [[Alturon]] in which he was killed by [[Tooth and Claw]].

Velns, magma dragon, companion to [[Darios O'Mael]] until the dragon's death in [[Dun na Fal]] during the Three Kings War.
