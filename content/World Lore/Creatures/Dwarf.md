Dwarves have an innate connection to the earth. Their skin tends to take on a rocky texture and appearance based on the stones that were nearby during their birth. In addition, dwarven bodies do not rot after death - 

Typically, dwarves get through their long lives (a few centuries, on average) by a strong sense of commitment, be that to a cause, a craft, or sometimes a person. This leads them to be seem by outsiders as stubborn, but also gives them their reputation as master crafters and powerful warriors.
