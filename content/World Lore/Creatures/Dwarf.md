As a species adapted to living underground, Dwarves have an innate connection to the earth. Their skin tends to take on a rocky texture and appearance based on the stones that were nearby during their birth. In addition, dwarven bodies do not rot after death - they petrify. 

Typically, dwarves get through their long lives (a few centuries, on average) by a strong sense of commitment, be that to a cause, a craft, or sometimes a person. This leads them to be seem by outsiders as stubborn, but also gives them their reputation as master crafters and powerful warriors. 

The homeland of the dwarves, once located in [[Avhalae]], was completely destroyed over a thousand years ago by the rise of the [[Blight]] - leading most of their people to flee to [[Antir]]. Echoes of this loss can still be felt in their culture, and many dwarves commit their lives to preserving their people's history and traditions.
