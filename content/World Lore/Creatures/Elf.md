#creature
Elves, despite common reputation, are actually mortal. However, their centuries-long lifespan lends them a perspective separate from most other peoples. The majority of elves who live among other mortals find that after a few centuries, the constant cycles of growth and loss become overwhelming. Most of these elves eventually retire to elven communities or find peace in solitude.

Many elves have a fondness for and connection to nature, given the slow and long-lasting nature of plant life. This also lends some a powerful connection to the spirits.
