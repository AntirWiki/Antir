Halflings are short, hirsute mortals commonly found in the cities and towns of [[Antir]]. They tend to have large families and short lifespans, and are found all across the continent in many walks of life.

Halflings are native to [[Avhalae]], but the majority fled to Antir when their lands fell to the undead. Given their shorter lifespans and general disregard for history and legacy, little knowledge remains about the nature of their homeland.
