Humans are the most numerous ancestry of mortals on [[Antir]] and [[Avhalae]], and can also be found on [[Taluvhas]].

They are most known for their industriousness and ingenuity, especially their ability to work in groups towards a shared goal.

Having a shorter lifespan than [[Dwarf|Dwarves]] and [[Elf|Elves]], many humans have a preoccupation with legacy and the establishment of institutions that will outlive them.

They are native to the continent of [[Avhalae]], though many fled from those lands when the undead [[Blight]] invaded. Most went over land to [[Antir]], but some fled by boat to the shores of [[Taluvhas]].

While most of the Avhali refugees went south through the [[Everweald]] into Antir, a small group of humans split off and went east - eventually settling among the [[Hallit]] of the mountains.
