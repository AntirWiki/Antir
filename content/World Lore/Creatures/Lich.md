A lich is a spellcaster who has undergone a terrible ritual to bind their soul to the mortal plane and allow the infinite regeneration of their undead body, making themselves effectively immortal.

# Reliquary
Lich's souls are bound to an object known as a *reliquary*, and if a lich's physical form is sufficiently damaged, their soul will retreat to the reliquary. Once in this state, a lich will typically wait until they've collected enough power to generate a new body. If a reliquary is destroyed (which can be done by magical means or with great physical damage), the lich's soul will return to their body, and their next death will be permanent unless they can bind a new reliquary before then. If there is no body to return to, the lich will die.

Some liches keep their reliquary on their person, as an important object like a staff, amulet, or spell book. Others hide their reliquaries in a secure location such as a tomb, dungeon, or keep.
