Story is a pixie who has been seen across the planes playing many roles. Her loyalties are unclear, and she seems to go wherever her whimsy takes her.

# Description
As a pixie, story has a small stature and gossamer wings. She has pale skin, short rose-colored hair, and three light-colored eyes. She's often seen wearing a short white dress, decorated with lace and flowers.
