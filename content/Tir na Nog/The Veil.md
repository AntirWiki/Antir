The Veil is a realm of [[Tir na Nog]] where dreams and possibilities mix. It is filled with visions of possible pasts, presents, and futures, both likely and unimaginable. Many scholars believe that mortal minds enter the Veil at least partially when they dream.

The Veil obeys no mortal laws of physics, and both time and space move strangely there. One can move between visions by walking, waiting, or even by thinking in specific patterns. The mechanisms that decide which realities appear in the Veil are completely enigmatic.

Mortal prophets of The Veil use structured dreaming or intoxicating substances to gain visions of the plane. This allows them to see possible futures, alongside pasts and presents that could have been. The information they gather can be incredibly insightful, but is often filled with far-fetched possibilities or even complete fabrications. In almost all cases, frequent exposure to the Veil leads to madness and an inability to perceive true reality.
