Ancestral enemies of the [[Hallit]], competing for resources in the northern mountains.

# Locations
While the kingdom has recently begun expanding, the vast majority of its population is still housed in the capital city. The new settlements have mostly been founded outside Antir, to the north and east, within the same mountain range.

# People
The kingdom's population is made up exclusively of the owl-like [[Moondancer|Moondancers]]. Their society is generally intolerant of other ancestries, even other birdfolk.

Matrilineal Religious Monarchy
