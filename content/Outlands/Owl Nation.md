The nation of the owlkin [[Moondancer|Moondancers]] sits atop one of the highest mountains in the northeastern corner of [[Antir]]. Their many-tiered city state is almost impossible to access without flight, and thus is almost impossible to access by any other than its native inhabitants.

They are ancestral enemies of the [[Hallit]], with whom they have been competing for resources in the northern mountains for generations.

# Locations
While the kingdom has recently begun expanding, the vast majority of its population is still housed in the capital city. The new settlements have mostly been founded outside Antir, to the north and east, within the same mountain range.

# People
The kingdom's population is made up exclusively of the owl-like [[Moondancer|Moondancers]].

# Culture
The Moondancers are known to be isolationist and intolerant of other mortals, even other birdfolk.

Their native language is called Moonsong.

# Government
Matrilineal Religious Monarchy
