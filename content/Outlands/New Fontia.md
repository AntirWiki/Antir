A ruined refugee city and monastery created in the [[Antir|Antiran]] mountains when the [[Font|Fontian]] fled The [[Lucentia]]. Protected by wards, staves, and the threat of the [[Demon Hunter#Wulvs|Wulvs]] surrounding it, this city has remained difficult to find–although a few Antiran scholars have made it inside and out to tell its tale. The city fell to [[Archivaldo]], and the people of New Fontia became refugees once again, and spread out into Antir. The consequences are still being felt, as those capable of [[Defied|defying]] spread out into Antir.
