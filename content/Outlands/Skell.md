The Skell are the native residents of the mountains south of [[Antir]], bordering [[Taluvhas]].

Much like the [[Hallit]] to the north, the Skell are mostly made up of [[Human|Humans]], [[Orc]], and Beastfolk.

The Skell and Hallit tribes traditionally meet once every eight years to exchange stories, goods, spiritual knowledge, and even people. The location of these meetings alternates between the southern mountains of the Skell and the northeastern mountains of the Hallit, with one group making a great journey to the other's homeland.
