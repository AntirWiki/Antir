The Hallit are the native residents of the mountains north of [[Antir]].

Much like the [[Skell]] to the south, the Hallit tribes are mostly made up of Humans, [[Orc]], and Beastfolk. The human tribes were formed by groups who separated from the original [[Avhalae|Avhali]] refugees as they passed through the [[Everweald]].

The Hallit are ancestral enemies of the [[Owl Nation]] due to generations of competition over territory and resources.
