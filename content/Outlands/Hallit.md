The Hallit are the native residents of the mountains north of [[Antir]].

Much like the [[Skell]] to the south, the Hallit tribes are mostly made up of [[Human|Humans]], [[Orc]], and Beastfolk. The human tribes were formed by groups who separated from the original [[Avhalae|Avhali]] refugees as they passed through the [[Everweald]].

The Hallit are ancestral enemies of the [[Owl Nation]] due to generations of competition over territory and resources.

The Hallit and Skell tribes traditionally meet once every eight years to exchange stories, goods, spiritual knowledge, and even people. The location of these meetings alternates between the southern mountains of the Skell and the northeastern mountains of the Hallit, with one group making a great journey to the other's homeland.
