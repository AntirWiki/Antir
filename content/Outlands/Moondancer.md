The owlkin (also known as Owlfolk or Moondancers) are an ancestry of beastfolk native to the mountains north of Antir. The vast majority of owlkin reside in the [[Owl Nation]].

The [[Hallit]], their ancestral enemies and territorial rivals, refer to them as "Todesschatten", which translates to "Death Shadow" - since their shadow is often the first (and last) sign that a Moondancer is hunting from above.
# Biology

![[Feral Owlfolk.png|400]]
Owlkin have wings across their arms, with hands at the ends much like a bat. As a result, they have extremely developed upper body muscles to allow for flight. This also leads to an underdeveloped lower body, able to stand, walk, and leap but unable to run or hike long distances.

Female owlfolk have wider hips and fluffier necks - leads to members of all genders deliberately fluffing up their necks.
