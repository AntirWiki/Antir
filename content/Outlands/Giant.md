Giants are a reclusive people who live beyond [[Antir]]'s eastern borders. They look similar in build to humans, but range from 12 to 50 feet tall.

Giants require an incredible amount of food to survive, so their families are small and they do not often congregate. There is only a single giant city, and it has very few permanent residents - most giants there are visitors, and leave once their task in the city is complete.

It is said that, due to the vast resources required to raise a giant to adulthood, the killing of another giant is the greatest taboo in their culture.
