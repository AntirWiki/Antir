Minotaur are cow-like beastfolk native to the mountains north of [[Antir]]. They have a bovine head on a furry bipedal body, and typically stand around 12 feet tall.

%%
Minotaurs are cursed with paranoia, and a feeling of never being safe. This is why they build mazes and traps and are constantly moving. Even in the mazes, they don't keep still, they are constantly maneuvering through the mazes, as a way to stay safe from, whatever it is they may be in danger from.

While they do maintain and form relationships needed for a society to exist, it is only with those closest relationships (namely immediate family) that they generally feel comfortable putting any confidence. This extends to always needing to hide, to move, to obscure. 

Side effects first set in after a minotaur has stayed in the same location for a few days. It materializes first as nightmare, vague impressions of fear plaguing their sleep. It shortly after progresses to more violent night terrors, with the minotaur shrieking and thrashing in their sleep as the effects increase.
%%

# Undermazes
Minotaurs almost all live in the enigmatic labyrinths known as **undermazes**, most buried deep under mountains.
%%
All the Undermazes are theoretically connected. At the heart of Minotaur territory is a structure known as the Evermaze. It is ancient. No one knows who built it. Even the Stonesmiths do not know how to navigate it, and those who enter do not return. There is a religious rite that involves a pilgrimage into the Evermaze, but its understood there is no return from such a journey
%%

# Culture
Minotaur rarely leave their mazes, so their society is largely a mystery to the rest of Antir.

Given their large stature and proximity to [[Giant]] lands, their culture has inherited many Giant values.

## Religion
They worship an equally enigmatic god known as the [[Lord of Labyrinths]].

The Stonesmiths are the only minotaurs that can fully see in the dark, can communicate with the other Minotaur stonesmiths, and can communicate directly with the Lord of Labyrinths. Stonesmiths are marked at birth for their station, with bright glowing _green_ eyes. They are the only magic users among the minotaurs. The priests (name pending?) are more mundane. They communicate with the masses on behalf of the Stonesmith and work to ease the Minotaur paranoia. There is a religious aspect of things, but I haven't figured that out yet. The Stonesmiths are the ones who plot out the paths of the Undermaze.
