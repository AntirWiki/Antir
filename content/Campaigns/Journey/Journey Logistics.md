Rations are cheap and light enough that you don't need to worry about tracking them. That's not to say they won't be relevant, but we will assume that barring exceptional circumstances you always buy a sufficient amount to make it to the next town.

Encumbrance does matter - if you have too much stuff, you'll need to find a way to carry it (horse, cart, etc). Fortunately Pathbuilder tracks it for you, so just make sure your inventory in there is accurate.
