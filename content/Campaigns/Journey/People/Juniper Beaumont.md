Juniper Beaumont is an acolyte of the [[Antiran Pantheon|Lucentian]] church in the town of [[Elmhollow]]. Their parents are the founders and priests of the church, and their sister [[Willow Beaumont]] is also an acolyte.

They are well liked by the people of Elmhollow, even those not involved with the church. They're a devoted acolyte to [[Lumina]], and most members of the congregation expect that they will take over the church when their parents retire.

# Description
Juniper is a half-elf with pale freckled skin, green eyes, and red hair that's shoulder-length in the back and shorter on the sides and front. They are not especially tall, and have an average build.

# GM Notes
[[Kyrillos]]' influence imprinted on them during the Flashback

Starting a few months before the campaign, Kyrillos can feel the Autumn King's rise in power (from his attempted apotheosis) and is trying to escape to take advantage of it. He has been occasionally grabbing Juniper, using their body (in the tree armor) to drain the life from nearby people, then letting Juniper go so he has time to absorb the power and work on breaking his bonds. Juniper has no memories of these events, but has noticed that they're "sleepwalking", waking up exhausted with aches and scratches on the western edge of town.

During one of these armored excursions they encountered [[Kirturok Stonehoof|Rock]] and, under Kyrillos' command, gravely wounded him.

Through Juniper, Kyrillos knows that the party are the strongest individuals in Elmhollow, and most likely to stop Juniper. To get them out of the way, he subtly influences Juniper to gather them all and distract them while he makes his final escape from the woods by possessing Juniper.

As an act of vengeance against those who he sees as his captors, he makes Juniper go to the church to destroy it - but isn't prepared for the power of Father Beaumont's faith.
