Yarrow Nightingale was a [[Witch|Wisdom]] who lived around the western [[Strioc an Athraithe]].

Her father was [[Hawthorne Nightingale]], renowned wizard of [[Foxfire Academy]]. She chose not to follow in her father's footsteps, instead learning witchcraft and nature magic to serve the common folk.

She eventually began a relationship with [[Kuruk]], the fae guardian of the [[Ravenwood]]. She became pregnant with his child, [[Thistle Nightingale]], but died during Thistle's birth around 1350 BR.
