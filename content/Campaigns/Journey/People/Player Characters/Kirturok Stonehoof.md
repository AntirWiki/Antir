Kirturok Stonehoof, also known as Rock, is an [[Adventurer]] known for fighting monsters across Antir.

# Description
Rock is not especially large for a [[Minotaur]], but still towers over most Antirans at 11' 5" (without the horns). Like most of his kin, he has a furred body, large hooves, and a face with a mix of human and bovine features. He has pronounced mutton chops and medium-length brown hair.

# History
Rock was a priest for the minotaur god the [[Lord of Labyrinths]]. He wasn't particularly pious, but he was decently intelligent, and his big size helped to inspire people in matters of faith. His eyes did not glow for his whole life. He lived what would he considered a simple life, serving his maze and his god as he was taught to do. Rock had a wife and a young child. His wife was VERY pious, and was a firm believer in the divinity of the Puzzlemaster. She was close with the Stonesmith. Somehow, the Stonesmith of Emerald Undermaze convinced Rock's wife and daughter to take the pilgrimage into the Evermaze behind Rock's back. By the time he learned about this, they were long gone. In his rage, Rock kills the Stonesmith, which leads to his exile.

Rock still has minotaur compulsion to constantly run and hide, and so he begins to wander Antir, with nothing but his clothes and his hammer. He doesn't seek out to be a monster hunter, it just sort of begins around him. Rock still does his prayers to the Lord of Labyrinths. Its a simple comfort, as part of his old life. He hates the Puzzlemaster, he understands this now, hence part of his quest becoming to kill god. Somewhere along his new life of hunting hags, daemons, and monsters, Rock's eyes started glowing blue. He has no earthly reason why.

He joins the Guild for a bit. This is probably when Rock's ego and reputation start to form as he becomes _very_ popular with different adventure parties on account of being giant and scary. Idk why the divorce happens yet. 

From there he travels to Catsi Crossing, almost immediately beefs with one of the Witches and leaves, because he thinks they're freaky. 

He spends a lot of time in Athraithe, traveling from village to village helping as he can, fighting off the spooky shit in the silent wood. He definitely visits [[Elmhollow]] multiple times throughout the years, largely checking on [[Willow Beaumont]], [[Juniper Beaumont]], and [[Thistle Nightingale]].

Rock learns about something emerging from the silent woods and taking/killing people from nearby roads and towns. The victims had small wounds, but none lethal - as if the life was simply drained from them. Rock searches for the killer, and eventually finds it - and is utterly defeated by the huge humanoid monster he finds. Left for dead, this is the moment that his Exemplar abilities manifest, keeping him alive long enough to stumble his way to Elmhollow.

# Timeline
Years 1-25: Born an ordinary minotaur minotaur in the Emerald Undermaze, located some distance north of the Athraithe village of Elmhollow. Largely unremarkable, save for his larger than average size. 

Year 26: Develops a relationship with the Stonesmith of the Emerald Undermaze, ultimately leading to his joining the priesthood of the Lord of Labyrinths

Years 27-39: Serves his people as priest to the Puzzlemaster, guiding them through the troubles of the minotaurs’ curse. During this time, Rock meets the woman who would later become his wife, later resulting in a healthy baby girl. Rock's wife was devoutly religious, more so than Rock.

Years 40: At the insistence of the Stonesmith, without Rock's knowledge, Rock's wife and daughter embark on a pilgrimage into the Evermaze, said to be the Labyrinth connecting all of minotaur-kind. It is also a death sentence, as none in the known history of the Emeralf undermaze has ever been seen again after entering the Evermaze. Upon discovering this, Rock killed the Stonesmith in a rage with his mark of station, his hammer. Rock was escorted out of the Undermaze and exiled from minotaur kind forever. Shortly after, Rock was taken in the Beaumonts in Elmhollow, wherein the events of Campaign 0 occurred. Shortly after the incident with Willow, Rock left the village, eventually finding himself in Dragun Mac Tire.

It was somewhere during this time that Rock swore to kill the Lord of Labyrinths. He places the blame for his family's demise, the darkness and fear of labyrinth life, and the curse of the minotaurs to never know safety and comfort on the Puzzlemaster, and would see the god dead for it.

Years 41-44: Rock joins the Adventurers Guild. This period sees extensive growth for him, both in stature and skill set. Rock quickly develops a reputation for himself as a great asset for Adventurers, due to his size and strength. He develops relationships with many members of Guild leadership, though this is not always positive. Eventually, his ego-driven conflict with guild leadership comes to a head after a solo monster hunting mission in the town of Belthanos results in the general destruction of the town, though he did save everyone involved from the monster harassing the town. This does ultimately result in Rock's departure from the Guild, when he punched his superior after he was chastised for this incident. The guild to the current day has not forgiven Rock for this. At some point during this period, Rock and Thistle travel together.

Year 45: Rock spends a brief period in Catsi Crossing, working as muscle for hire. However, he didn't mesh well with the general culture of the Witch Coven, and developed negative relationships with several prominent witches. 

Years 46-49: Rock continues to travel, offering his services to anyone across greater Antir in need, from Zauberei to Blackhawk to Lucentia to Athraithe. The reputation of the Bull of Belthanos often preceded him, for better or worse. He dedicated his life to the destruction of daemons, monsters, cults, hags, and all who would harm innocents. See below for random reputation generator.

Year 50: Rock is struck down in Athraithe by an unknown entity near Elmhellow. The injury is severe and nearly killed him, were it not for the assistance of his friends the Beaumonts. It is during this recovery period that Rock's eyes begin to glow a vibrant blue, similar in intensity to the yellow eyes of the Stonesmiths. Rock is uncertain why this is and does not care for it. Rock's recovery period is worsened by his inability to keep still due to the Minotaur curse.

# GM Notes
The creature that wounded Rock was [[Juniper Beaumont]], possessed by Kyrillos as the lich was trying to gather enough power to leave the Silent Wood.

His eyes have begun glowing blue because the Lord of Labyrinths has chosen him as a sort of anti-priest. As a god of eternal struggle, the Lord feels he has become complacent in his power. Therefore, he is cultivating Rock as a threat to himself. He supports Rock's endeavors during the Journey because he feels that stopping an apotheosis is a good stepping stone to posing a threat to an active god.
