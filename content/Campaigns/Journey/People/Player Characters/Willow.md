Willow is an acolyte of the [[Antiran Pantheon|Lucentian]] church in the town of [[Elmhollow]]. Her parents are the founders and priests of the church, and her sibling [[Juniper]] is also an acolyte.

# Description
Willow has long red hair and pale skin. She is usually seen wearing priestly garments that cover almost all of her body. She often wears a censer woven into her braided hair.

%%
# The Retting
The Retting is a spirit that has attached itself to Willow, granting her necromantic abilities. 

Formed by pure spiritual power, it begins with almost no personality - just an animal curiosity, and adapts to Willow's perception of it. At the start of the campaign it has finally grasped language, and has a sinister air - because Willow herself has such a negative perception of it.

The spirit is the remnants of the bond between [[Kyrillos]] and the [[Autumn King]], containing some of both of their powers but little of either mind. Both beings want to use it to finally consume the other and regain their full strength.

When Kyrillos moved to inhabit Juniper, the Autumn King used his influence to force the Retting to separate from him. As the only available vessel, it jumped to Willow.
