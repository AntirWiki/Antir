A group consisting of [[Kirturok Stonehoof]], [[Otto Von Geist]], [[Thistle Nightingale]], and [[Willow Beaumont]].

The four left [[Elmhollow]] in the summer of 1375, chasing after [[Juniper Beaumont]] who, wielding strange powers and wearing massive cursed armor, kidnapped town priest Sylas Beaumont.
