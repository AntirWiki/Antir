*Save this for higher level, so the fight lasts longer?*

The party arrives in a small town at night. The next morning, they're confronted by a loud, burly man. He shouts at Rock specifically, saying that he's owed a "rematch".

With a DC15 society check, Rock recalls that he fought this man last time he came this way - and that the man was an easy fight and a sore loser.

With a 20 or higher on the society check, he also remembers that the man accused Rock of being a "hallit shay-man" and of using "mountain voodoo" to cheat.

The man challenges Rock to a rematch, betting a significant amount of money.

If Rock declines, the man derides and insults him until the party leave town. If he accepts, the group is led outside into the street. A small crowd gathers, from the inn and nearby buildings.

The man has secretly enlisted the help of a local hedge mage to, as he sees it, level the playing field with Rock. The mage is hiding in the crowd, incanting under their breath to empower the fighter.

With the enchantments, the man is stronger than Rock. At some point in the fight, the fighter grabs Rock in a chokehold and mutters, "I've got some voodoo of my own this time, buffalo man".

If rock wins the fight despite the disadvantage, he gets the promised bet. If the party outs the cheating, the fighter and the mage are shamed by the crowd until they give up double the bet.
