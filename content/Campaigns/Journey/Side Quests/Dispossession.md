A (priest of Tival? [[Demon Hunter]]?) beseeches the party's aid infiltrating and destroying a cult. The cult is being led by a [[Defied]], and the quest giver wishes to exorcize the demon. They offer pay, and also to teach the party exorcism (which could be invaluable in saving Juniper?)

During the final confrontation, it is revealed that the Defied host was the quest-giver's father. They want to provide him with a true and peaceful death, free of demonic corruption.
