# Intro
>[!recite] In the Inn
>As you sit with your meal, a figure approaches and swiftly takes the fifth seat at the table. She's tall and dark-skinned, wearing a wide-brimmed hat and dark leather coat. As you react to her sudden appearance, she puts both arms on the table in a businesslike pose.

The woman is Francesca Ortiz, [[Demon Hunter]]. Her speech is blunt and emotionless to an extreme. She asks if the party are adventurers, then offers to hire them. She says that she is trying to perform an exorcism, and that her target is too well-defended for her to go alone.

She is trying to hire adventurers new to the area because she fears that her target may have recruited locals to his steadily-growing cult. 

She refuses to give the party any more information until they agree to assist. When they do, she gives the details: a [[Defied]] going by the name Andras is growing a cult in nearby ruins, and she seeks to exorcize him before any serious damage is done. The cult has already burned down the local church of Lumina. She needs the party to help her break into the compound, aid in taking down the demon, and help in the ritual to exorcize him.

She tells the party to meet her an hour before dusk the next day, since she can only guarantee the Defied's presence during the cult's nightly meetings.

# Preparations
Meeting the party, Francesca goes over the plan in more detail.

She intends to fight her way through the ruins, to the chapel where Andras holds court. Once there, the party must defeat him without killing him. This will allow her to perform the exorcism, which requires three participants.

She is reluctant to explain why Andras mustn't be killed, but tells the party that half of their payment depends on him surviving until the ritual. The truth is that Andras is possessing the body of her late father, and she fears what would happen to his soul if the demon is slain in his body.

# The Ruins

# Andras
Andras has deep purple skin and a voice that is both booming and velvety smooth.

When combat begins, Francesca says she will deal with him personally while the party holds off his court. The cultists attack, while she and Andras move to the upper floors of the ruined temple to fight. The party occasionally sees flashes of their battle through the stained glass windows and broken walls, hearing gunshots firing off at an incredible rate.

When the party has nearly finished off the cultists, Andras throws Francesca through the stained glass window above the altar. She slides to the ground, unable to stand, and he steps down - wounded, but still in fighting shape. Francesca throws her Astral rune to a player and begs them to finish the job.
