A [[Demon Hunter]] named Francesca Ortiz beseeches the party's aid infiltrating and destroying a cult. The cult is being led by a [[Defied]], and the quest giver wishes to exorcize the demon. She offers pay, and also to teach the party exorcism (which could be invaluable in saving Juniper?)

Francesca is a [[Demon Hunter#Wulvs|Wulf]] host, and warns the party that if she is hurt during the conflict she may "change". She gives the party a passphrase - "May the Font shine golden" - to prove they're allies if a Wulf emerges.

If she is killed, she transforms into a Wulf named Andromeda. Andromeda - or Andy - has dark skin like Francesca, but a slightly heavier build. When she transforms, she picks up Francesca's pistol - and the gun transforms into a hulking shotgun inscribed with golden runes. Andy has no memories of Francesca, and will immediately try to kill the Defied if she isn't stopped.

During the final confrontation, it is revealed that the Defied host was Francesca's father. She wants to provide him with a true and peaceful death, free of demonic corruption.

# Intro
>[!recite] In the Inn
>As you sit with your meal, a figure approaches and swiftly takes the sixth seat at the table. She's tall and dark-skinned, wearing a wide-brimmed hat and dark leather coat. As you react to her sudden appearance, she puts both arms on the table in a businesslike pose.

The woman is Francesca Ortiz, [[Demon Hunter]]. She asks if the party are adventurers, then offers to hire them. She says that she is trying to perform an exorcism, and that her target is too well-defended for her to go alone.

She is trying to hire adventurers new to the area because she fears that her target may have recruited locals to his steadily-growing cult. 

She refuses to give the party any more information until they agree to assist. When they do, she gives the details: a [[Defied]] going by the name Andras is growing a cult in nearby ruins, and she seeks to exorcize him before any serious damage is done. The cult has already burned down the local church of Lumina. She needs the party to help her break into the compound, aid in taking down the demon, and help in the ritual to exorcize him.

She tells the party to meet her an hour before dusk the next day, since she can only guarantee the Defied's presence during the cult's nightly meetings.

# Preparations
Meeting the party, Francesca goes over the plan in more detail.

She intends to fight her way through the ruins, to the basement where Andras holds court. Once there, the party must defeat him without killing him. This will allow her to perform the exorcism, which requires three participants.

She is reluctant to explain why Andras mustn't be killed, but tells the party that half of their payment depends on him surviving until the ritual. The truth is that Andras is possessing the body of her late father, and she fears what would happen to his soul if the demon is slain in his body.

She asks the party to finish the job if she is killed, showing them her journal with notes on how to complete the ritual. Finally, she warns them that if she is hurt during the conflict she may "change". She gives the party a passphrase - "May the Font shine golden" - to prove they're allies in case she doesn't recognize them afterwards. No amount of persuasion will convince her to share more information about the change.
