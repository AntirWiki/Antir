In the Kingdom, near the border with athraithe, the party finds a town preparing for winter. Their local wisdom, a woman with pure white eyes that they call "The Seer" has predicted winter's arrival in about one week. This would be a good chance to show what it looks like when Kingdomers prepare for the sudden winter. 

The town is in a panic because all their food, which they have been stockpiling over the long summer, has been stolen. The party shares their sentiments - all their food was stolen the night before they made it to town. The townsfolk are sympathetic, but have no food to give the party. Unless they want to hunt their way to Alturon, they'll have to figure out where all this food is going.

Ultimately it's discovered that a dragon (young forest dragon?) has been watching the town for a while and decided that since the townsfolk seem to spend *so* much time gathering food, it must be very valuable. So it has decided to start hoarding food. It can possibly be convinced to part with its horde with clever negotiation ("If you let the town hold onto your horde and eat it sometimes, they'll willingly bring you more every year!")
