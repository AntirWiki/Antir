A healer has recently arrived in a town south of Alturon.

They are using powers granted by the Autumn King to temporarily heal people, which later causes them to wither away completely. They have already disposed of their first victim, burying them behind their house (there is a large patch of disturbed snow/earth that may be suspicious). They wrote a note from this person claiming they were so happy to be healed they set off on a journey.

They offer to heal Thistle's wings.
