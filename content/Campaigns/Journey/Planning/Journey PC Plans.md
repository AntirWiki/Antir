# What is each character good at? How can they use it?
**Rock -** 
- Fighting, knows the road, knows gods
- Helping with travel, shutting down the Autumn King's apotheosis
**Willow -** 
- Knows religion, affinity with undeath
- Finding Kyrillos' weaknesses, learning the Autumn King's plan
**Thistle -** 
- Knows spirits, the Guild, adventuring
- Getting the group official support, helping during travel
**Otto -** 
- Knows Demons, knows possession, knows how to hunt
- Hunting Kyrillos and his minions, freeing Juniper

# Plots
**Rock -** Earning titles from the Lord of Labyrinths, stopping an evil god
**Willow -** Accepting or banishing the Retting, and figuring out her faith
**Thistle -** Rescuing Juniper, saving their father
**Otto -** Learning about Vada's plans and choosing to accept or reject that

# Deaths
**Rock** - The Lord of Labyrinths brings him back, but as a servant instead of a rival
**Willow** - The Retting brings her back with necromancy, immediately alerting Juniper/Kyrillos/the King to the party's current location and activities
**Thistle** - Father sacrifices himself?
**Otto** - Vada brings them back - reincarnated into their own body - but in doing so, gives up her own claim. It means it will be decades before she can re-manifest, and Otto's next death will give him to Athanasius
