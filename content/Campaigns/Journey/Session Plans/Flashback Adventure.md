# Introduction
Young Thistle, Willow, and Juniper are playing outside. Rock Willow's cat runs into the forest, and Juniper insists they give chase.

Otto, who has been left in Elmhollow while Vada takes on a dangerous hunt, sees this group run into the cursed woods. Either they're children about to be in danger, or they're doing something suspicious - regardless, he'd best follow after them.

# Into the Woods
The woods are dark, and there's clearly something following them - a large dark shadow flits between the trees in the corner of one's eye.

After a bit of walking, all players must make fortitude saves (DC10) or become Drained 1.

Eventually, they come upon a strange mushroom circle. If they step into it, the fight starts with surprise - otherwise, the myconid elder tries to sneak up on them - but the group are alerted by a loud, uncanny raven call.

During the fight, one of the creatures goes for Willow and is eliminated by a large dark shadow that swiftly flies down from the trees, grabs the mushroom, and flies off.

It's clear that the tallest one was once a person - the fungus is grown over a skeleton, still wearing strange jewelry.

# The Tree
The party finds themselves in a clearing, with a massive twisted oak in the center. The other trees leave it a wide berth, as if afraid to be touched by its reaching, spiked branches with ends like clawed hands.

Juniper sees the cat nestled in a hollow of the tree's roots, and rushes forward - completely missing the corpses of people and animals that rise up around them.

The enemies block anyone but Willow and Juniper from approaching the tree. Juniper rushes to the cat, but as they step forward to grab it, are suddenly caught in the tree's roots.

When Willow grabs their hand to help them free, she feels a chill travel up her arm and through her body. Juniper cries out, and falls unconscious - though as they fall, they're no longer stuck at all.

At this point the undead stop strategizing, instead just attacking mindlessly. More and more arise until the party grabs Juniper, Willow grabs the cat, and they flee.

# End of the Beginning
The group makes their way out of the forest unimpeded. Shaken by the experience, where does each person go?
