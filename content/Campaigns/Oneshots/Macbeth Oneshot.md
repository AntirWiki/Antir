Characters:
1. The Captain
	1. Description: The leader of the Duke's military, sworn to fight and die for them.
	2. Intro: During the last war, you saved the Duke's life. Who did you have to kill?
	3. Goal: You cannot live with the guilt of what you've done. Die with honor.
2. The Witch
	1. Description: Maiden, Mother, or Crone; Witch, Wisdom, or Hag. Your coven seeks more power over this kingdom, and the Duke presents the best means of gaining it.
	2. Intro: What did you sacrifice for your power? *Wait for answer.* Was it worth it?
	3. Goal: A broken mind is easier to manipulate. Ensure the Duke is completely mad when they are crowned
3. The Priest
	1. Description: The kingdom's spiritual advisor, a zealot with a haunted past.
	2. Intro: What tragedy drove you to the cloth?
	3. Goal: Heresy will corrupt the kingdom from the inside out. The Witch must die, but none can suspect that you're responsible.
4. The Confidante
	1. Description: Lover or friend, parent or child, you are someone very close to the Duke. The Duke trusts you and listens to you, and you do not hesitate for a moment to take advantage of this.
	2. Intro: How did you convince the Duke to forsake morals for ambition?
	3. Goal: This will only be worth it if the duke still trusts and listens to you by the end. When they are crowned, the Duke must still be sane.
5. The Duke
	1. Description: The Duke.
	2. Intro: How did you kill the king?
	3. Goal: Either survive, or take the other four down with you.

Mechanics:
- Death ability
- "Haunt the narrative" action that can be taken a certain number of times while dead
