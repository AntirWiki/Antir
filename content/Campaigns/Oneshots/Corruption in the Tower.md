Final encounter:

>[!recite] Entering the Summoning Chamber
> You hear voices just before you enter the chamber.
> 
> "Find one of the intruders, and spill their blood upon the circle. Then we will be-" The voice is interrupted as you step into the room.
> 
> There you see two figures, standing over a strange circle, illuminated by candlelight. One appears to be another cultist, in the black fox mask. The other, whose hand rests on his shoulder, appears to be the same - before you realize that there is no mask, there. This figure, standing tall in a blood red robe, has the face of a black-furred fox. Strange growths - like horns, or antennae - sprout from its head, twisting above its hood. It looks to you with surprise, then back to its cultist.
> 
> "Oh, hells. Well, I suppose you'll do." In an instant, the hand that rested on the cultist's shoulder swipes across his throat. The man falls to his knees, blood spilling out onto a complex sigil inscribed on the ground.
> 
> Then, as the blood fills the sigil's lines, it begins to glow. A massive hand appears to burst from the brick floor, grabbing the fresh corpse and pulling it under with a *crunch*. Then it re-emerges, followed by another, followed by a horned head and a densely muscled torso. The beast finishes climbing through the stonework - which is somehow unbroken, now - and stands almost 12 feet tall, horns brushing the cieling.
> 
> "Kill them," says the fox-creature casually.
> 
> Roll initiative
