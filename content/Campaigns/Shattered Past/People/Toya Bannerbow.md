Toya Bannerbow was a general of [[Cianan of Antir]]'s army during the [[Three Kings War]]. They continued to lead troops after the war, notably assisting with defensive coordination during the 953 BR [[Alturon#Siege of 953|Siege of Alturon]].

They were an excellent archer, and their surname came from a battlefield tradition: each time they shot down an enemy commander, they took a strip of their banner and tied it to their longbow. By the end of the war, the wood of the bow was barely visible under the cloths.

# Description
Toya was a tall lean catfolk with short black fur.
