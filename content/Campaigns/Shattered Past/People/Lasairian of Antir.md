Lasairian of Antir was the eldest child of King Tarlach of Antir, elder brother to [[Faolan of Antir]], [[Cianan of Antir]] and [[Aisling of Antir]]. 

In his middle years, he served under his father as a general of Antir's military.

During the [[Three Kings War]], he led the southern lands against his brothers. Over the course of the war, he allied with the [[Dragon|dragons]] of the southern mountains, making political and magical pacts with them. He was magically bonded with his greatest ally among them, a black dragon known as Ozymandias.

He was killed by Faolan during the final battle of the war, during which he is rumored to have transformed into a twisted half-dragon form.

# On an Alliance with Dragons
*The following speech was scribed in 949 BR, three years into the Three Kings War*

"My brothers, my sisters, my comrades. I am sure all of you have heard rumors of my absence. I know that these tales spread that I was abandoning you, abandoning our cause, out of some petty fear. But I stand before you now to tell you that we are beyond fear. Fear is a thing beneath us, something that we have ground beneath our boots and left behind with the people we used to be.

Fear is for those who exiled us from our rightful lands, the weak lapdogs of the capital baying at my brother's feet. We have been forced out of our homes, separated from our families, because of the whims of the nobility and the rich. Because they cannot understand *true strength*, they cannot understand what it means to be a true Antiran. They fear us, they fear what we represent, and so we are driven away like animals.

And we are not the first who have received this brutality from the misguided whims of nobility. I went away to find those who have suffered the same injustice as us, those who have been forced into hiding for generations by the petty *fear* of people below them. People below us.

This is the first of their kind that I met."

At this, an illusion slowly drops, and the form of a massive Dragon shimmers into view, standing next to Lasairian. The dragon is slender, with shining black scales that come to sharp points all across its body. It towers almost 20 feet tall, looming over the collected soldiers and eyeing them with clear intelligence. Gasps are heard from the crowd, and several soldiers begin unsheathing blades.

"Hold, my comrades. This is not an enemy. This is the first of the allies who will win us this war. Ozymandias and I traveled throughout the mountains, speaking with Dragons who had been forced from their homes by the same people who forced us from ours. And in those travels, we found *power*."

As he speaks this word, great black wings extend from his back. The sharp spines make a clear connection to the wings resting on Ozymandias's back.

"The bond between myself and Ozymandias has given both of us power the likes of which this world has never seen. But this power is not mine alone. No, it is ours. For Ozymandias is not the only one of his people to find our cause worthy."

At this there is a commotion in the crowd, as some spot shapes moving towards them in the sky. Moments later, five dragons of all different shapes, sizes, and colors land on the stage in front of Lasairian's raised platform. Before them, five figures step from the crowd.

"Your generals will be the first of many to follow me in forging this bond. This day will be marked in history as the day the Dragons allied with the true Antirans to reclaim our homes! Reunite with our families! And take back Antir!"
