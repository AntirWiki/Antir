Brennan Tydell was the captain of the Antiran royal guard around the time of the [[Three Kings War]].

He was the uncle of royal heirs [[Lasairian of Antir|Lasairian]], [[Faolan of Antir|Faolan]], [[Cianan of Antir|Cianan]], and [[Aisling of Antir]], his sister being their mother.

# Description
Brennan was a tall, well-muscled half-elf with pale skin and short cut red hair. He was typically seen in the brass armor of the royal guard.

He wielded an enchanted longsword, a family heirloom. It had the ability to extend to nearly eight feet long, and the blade could become dangerously cold on command.
