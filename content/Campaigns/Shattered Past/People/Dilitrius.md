Dilitrius was a green [[dragon]] who allied with [[Lasairian of Antir]] during the [[Three Kings War]] and attempted to overthrow [[Cianan of Antir]] after the war's conclusion.

He was defeated by [[Tooth and Claw]] in 953 BR during the Siege of [[Alturon]], briefly falling under the control of Cianan before the king had him killed.

# Description
Dilitrius had emerald green scales and a serpentine form with four small legs. He had the ability to fly despite lacking wings. He was approximately 30 feet long when he was killed.
