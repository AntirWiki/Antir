Miriel Tydell was the royal librarian of the [[Sunlit Palace]] in [[Alturon]].

She was the maternal grandmother to royal heirs [[Lasairian of Antir|Lasairian]], [[Faolan of Antir|Faolan]], [[Cianan of Antir|Cianan]], and [[Aisling of Antir]].

# Description
Miriel was a pale, red-haired elf who wore a white and green robe.
