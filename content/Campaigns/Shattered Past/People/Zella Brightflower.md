Zella Brightflower was a gnome wizard who resided in the outskirts of [[Zauberei]]. She was the adoptive mother of [[West Hemlocke]] and the arcane tutor of [[Cianan of Antir]].

She served as the headmaster of [[Foxfire Academy]] from its founding until Cianan and Hemlocke took over leadership in 965 BR.

Zella was unusually generous with her knowledge, often having several apprentices at once. This may have inspired Prince Cianan's creation of Foxfire Academy during the [[Three Kings War]].

# Description

Zella is a gnome with tan skin and large, curly, fire-red hair. She has a strict, matronly attitude.
