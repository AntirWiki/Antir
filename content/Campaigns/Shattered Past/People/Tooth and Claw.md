"Tooth and Claw" was the stage name of a group gathered by [[Cianan of Antir]] to quell the unrest around [[Alturon]] following the [[Three Kings War]]. 

The group consisted of royal bodyguard [[West Hemlocke]], prize fighter [[Valerie Bouchard]], former general [[Darios O'Mael]] and village Wisdom [[Ninyva O'Niamh]].

During their brief existence, the group freed the corrupted guardian spirit of [[Collach]], successfully prevented the return of [[Kyrillos]] the lich, killed the rogue [[General Tanner]], and defeated the green dragon [[Dilitrius]] when he attacked Alturon.

The group was disbanded immediately following Dilitrius' defeat, when Ninyva attempted to kill King Cianan using powers gained from [[Autumn King|The Lord of Gilded Leaves]]. The other three members then helped the king defeat and capture her. Afterwards, Valerie and Darios became adventurers, continuing to search for a way to undo Nini's fae corruption. Hemlocke eventually married Cianan, and the two later retired to become headmasters at [[Foxfire Academy]] in [[Zauberei]].
