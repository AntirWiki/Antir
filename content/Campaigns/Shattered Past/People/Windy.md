Wind Whispers Through Leaves (known to most as "Windy") was the royal jester of Antir in 953 BR. He was also, unknown to all, the kingdom's spymaster. He received and delivered messages to agents by posing as a messenger from the "real" spymaster, thus keeping his identity hidden.

# Description
Windy is a gourd leshy, with a large pumpkin head, vine limbs, and two large leaves growing from his shoulders, giving the impression of a cloak.
