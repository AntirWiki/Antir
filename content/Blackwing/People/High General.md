The current High General of the [[Blackwing Legion]] is a shrewd and calculating man, known for quietly and unmercifully outmaneuvering his foes.

When his position as High General is challenged, he typically chooses a game of chess for his contest. Rumor says that his room - or tent, on campaign - houses a chess board magically linked to Archmage [[Irina Varazs]] of [[Foxfire Academy]]. %%It is actually linked to Headmaster [[Miriam Varazs]] - Irina does not have the patience for chess.%%

# Description
He has dark skin and short hair that's just beginning to turn gray. He is usually seen in his signature plate mail, carrying what appears to be standard legionary sword and shield.
