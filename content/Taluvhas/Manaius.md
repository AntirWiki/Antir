Manaius is a region of arid steppes in the northeastern part of Taluvhas. It is one of the vassal states controlled by the [[Autumn King]], the last to fall to his conquest of the continent.

# Locations
There are no permanent settlements in Manaius, due to the everpresent threat of draconic attack.

# Government
Manaius is a vassal state to the [[Autumn King]].
 
Its nomadic clans are led by a noble class of centaurs.

# People
The Manaiuud are a nomadic people. They are renowned horse breeders and riders.

# History
The Manaiuud were not always nomadic. After the Dragon Wars in [[Antir]], a massive dragon settled in the country. The war had given it a fear of organized mortals, so it destroyed all settlements near its new home. When the people tried to repair their homes, it destroyed them again. This pattern continued, forcing the people into a life with no permanent settlements.

When this happened, the centaurs in the region - who already lived as nomads - offered to take them into their clans. This eventually led to the centaurs becoming the ruling class of the country.
