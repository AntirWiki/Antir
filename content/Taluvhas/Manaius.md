Manaius is a region of arid steppes in the northeastern part of Taluvhas. It is one of the vassal states controlled by the [[Autumn King]].

# Locations
There are no permanent settlements in Manaius, due to the everpresent threat of draconic attack.

# Government
Manaius is a vassal state to the [[Autumn King]].

# People
The Manaiuud are a nomadic people. They are renowned horse breeders and riders.

They were not always nomadic, but the incursion of the [[Dragon|Dragons]] into Taluvhas led to the destruction of their cities and forced them to constantly move to avoid the attention of the dragons.
