# CELTIC INSPIRED
%%

Avhalae is a continent west of [[Antir]], far across the ocean. It is the original home of humans, [[Elf|Elves]], [[Dwarf|Dwarves]], and [[Halfling|Halflings]].

It is plagued by an army of undead%% known as [[Undead Horde]]%%, and its people are locked in constant war with these living corpses. The horde's arrival over a thousand years ago ruined the original nations on the continent, sending many of its residents feeing to the [[Valley of Cold Sun]] (and later Antir). A group of humans also fled by boat to [[Taluvhas]].

# The Last Kingdom
The only nation to survive the millennia of assault, this last bastion houses the remnants of the continent's people. Their culture is grim and pragmatic, since the threat of annihilation is always just around the corner.

# The Elven Nation
The ancestral [[Elf|Elven]] homeland has recently been resettled by idealistic elves from [[Antir]], who seek to reclaim and rebuild their ancient nation. Thanks to their long lifespan, some of these elves are the children or grandchildren of those who lived before the [[Undead Horde]].

# The Dwarven Nations
The ancient homes of the [[Dwarf|Dwarves]] were completely destroyed when the [[Undead Horde]] appeared.
