# CELTIC INSPIRED
%%

Avhalae is a continent west of [[Antir]], far across the ocean. It is the original home of [[Human|Humans]], [[Elf|Elves]], [[Dwarf|Dwarves]], and [[Halfling|Halflings]].

It is plagued by an army of undead known as the [[Blight]], and its people are locked in constant war with these living corpses. The horde's arrival over a thousand years ago ruined the original nations on the continent, sending many of its residents feeing through the [[Valley of Cold Sun]] and into Antir. A group of humans also fled by boat to [[Taluvhas]].

# The Last Kingdom
The only nation to survive the millennia of assault, this last bastion houses the remnants of the continent's people. Their culture is grim and pragmatic, since the threat of annihilation is always just around the corner.

# The Elven Nation
The ancestral [[Elf|Elven]] homeland has recently been revived by idealistic elves from [[Antir]], who seek to reclaim and rebuild their ancient nation. Thanks to their long lifespan, some of these elves are the children or grandchildren of those who lived before the [[Blight]].

# The Dwarven Nations
The vast subterranean holds of the [[Dwarf|Dwarves]] were completely destroyed when the [[Blight]] appeared. Some believe that the caverns remain, and the majority of the undead horde hide within them.
