The Riverlands is a chaotic and dangerous region in southeastern [[Antir]]. It is most known for the large rivers that weave through the area, which regularly change course and alter the landscape. Because of this, the only permanent settlements that can be built in the Riverlands are along the borders.

The primary mortal residents of the Riverlands are the nomadic [[Shellfolk]] people. The area is also home to numerous monsters, which sometimes leave the region to menace the nearby [[Blackwing]] to the west and [[Holy Kingdom]] to the north.

The southern coast is home to the "dueling cities" [[Kaava]] and [[Vakash]], which sit on opposite sides of a large bay.
