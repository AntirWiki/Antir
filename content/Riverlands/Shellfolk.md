The Shellfolk are beastfolk native to the [[Riverlands]] of [[Antir]]. The ancestries within their nomadic tribes include shrimpfolk, crabfolk, and many other types of crustacean-kin. They also have an unusually high number of Awakened Animals in their number.

The shellfolk live in nomadic groups, creating temporary settlements of wood and mud that last a few months on average before being destroyed by the shifting rivers of their homeland. Some groups rotate between a number of known sites, while others roam the entire span of the riverlands.

Traditional shellfolk religion reveres the rivers themselves as great sleeping gods, who shift in their slumbers uncaring of the folk on land.
