Vakash is one of the Dueling Cities located south of the [[Riverlands]] in [[Antir]]. 

Vakash is located atop a high cliff overlooking the bay. The most notable geographical feature is a waterfall that flows backwards, shooting up from the ocean to the top of the cliff. Ships will use the falls to transport sealed barrels full of goods and particularly brave sailors up to the city.
