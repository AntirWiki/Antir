Vakash is one of the Dueling Cities located south of the [[Riverlands]] in [[Antir]]. It shares a bay with rival city [[Kaava]].

Vakash is located atop a high cliff overlooking the bay. The most notable geographical feature is a waterfall that flows backwards, shooting up from the ocean to the top of the cliff. Ships will use the falls to transport sealed barrels full of goods and particularly brave sailors up to the city - a much faster method than the mechanical lifts from the port to the city.

The city is known for its population of pirates and other criminals, since the vantage point of the cliffs makes it incredibly defensible against naval attacks from [[Blackwing]] or Kaavan privateers.
