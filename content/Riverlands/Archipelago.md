The islands in [[Antir|Antir]]'s southern bay are beholden to no mainland country, instead operating as a neutral force between Antir and [[Taluvhas]]. Though they shelter many piratical fleets, their power is mainly not military - instead, the threat of withholding trade from a nation is sufficient to get their way often enough.
