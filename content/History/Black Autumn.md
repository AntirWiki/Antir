The Black Autumn is a name for the autumn of 1077 BR, when the archfae known as [[The Autumn King]] and the [[lich]] [[Kyrillos]] attacked [[Alturon]], were defeated, and created [[The Silent Woods]]. 

>[!quote] From "The Hærfest Affairs"
The sky crackled green in bursts across Antir that night as Fey combined with Lich to create something both unholy and entirely unknown. The king had learned from what had gone wrong with Ninyva’s grand design, and had improved upon it. A great and ancient oak, the Lord’s prized possession within his domain, was to be given life & limb, and become the host. Kyrillos, however, had stretched his hands further into the Lord than his panic and pride had let him feel. So, when the last drops of consciousness from Kyrillos were gifted upon the tree, the Lich pulled upon his puppet strings and snatched the Lord in with him. _Thus, The Autumn King was born._ A monstrous uprooting as the tree both grew and died. Every step crushing entire neighborhoods, and leaving sharpened, decaying splinters of rotted and fetid wood to poison the earth it pierced into. Two minds unnaturally joined in a single form. The Lord’s agony mixed with the Lich’s unending Lust for Power. Much to Kyrillos’s terror, it was entirely impossible for either side to come out entirely on top. Instead, major fears and emotions guided the creature’s moments. It became a force of nondescriminate destruction, withering everything and everyone its wide and snaking roots could touch. The lord’s domain, and those within it, fell quickly and silently to an oppressive decay, A quiet, corrupted death for a great House, as if purposefully spurning the loud and prideful nature of its leader. Once the Lord’s domain was crumbled beyond recognition, the malaise spread….unidirectional, as if catching onto an idea battled within its twin-mind, and following it as order. It rotted away the barriers to the feywild gate near Catse Crossing, and gave the creature access to the mortal plane.
The Black Autumn fell upon Antir. Careening towards the city, the crown jewel of the empire…. Memories fought and tangled but the city shone like a pristine beacon to its befuddled mind. Alturon. The last place an altered grimoire & powerful fallen witch were both seen, the Autumn King burned with a lust to reclaim, and tainted everything in its wake to get there. It had to be in Alturon. The last step to becoming god...
...As the brave adventurers rallied on, the mages of Alturon scrambled to read & modify the rest of the scraps cobbled together from Corsair Molossus Valerie and Sir Darios Scalemaster’s old notes left discarded by Ninyva’s resting place. Eventually, the final line was drawn, and the seal was pressed into the city square proper. Symbols for Suppression repurposed into symbols for Separation. The alphabetical similarities were notable, but still different enough to pose substantial risks. Slowly, the beast was pushed towards the glowing runes and...
...After what is known today as “the Schism of Arcana”, those who were lost were cleansed by clerics of the Lucent and laid to rest. Those poor unfortunate souls stuck between the realms of living and dead, were carted off to some fate still unknown today.
